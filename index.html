Favourite Festivals

holi

The "Festival of Colors" or the "Festival of Love" is a celebration of the arrival of spring. In addition to the colorful celebrations, Holi is also a time to forgive and forget.

diwali

The festival of lights celebrated by lighting diyas and candles, decorating homes with rangolis, exchanging gifts, and preparing and sharing traditional sweets and dishes with family and friends.

basant

The kite festival is a lively and colorful event. The skies are filled with colorful kites of different shapes and sizes, and it is a truly mesmerizing sight.
